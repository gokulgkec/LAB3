Chairman's Message

Shri. A.M.KANDASWAMI Chairman Sasurie & Nyruthi Institutions Tiruppur, Coimbatore.

Education is the process of continuous growth and is lifelong. Educational Institutions should be the home and repository of fresh thoughts and new orientations. Whatever may be the transformation, it will be clear that education has two main aspects – the cultural aspects which make a person grow and, the production aspect which makes a person do things. These two aspects are not exclusive of each other. Education, science based and coherent with Indian culture and values, alone can provide the foundation for the nation’s security and welfare.

NSS (National Service Scheme)

National Service Scheme is one of the pathways to serve the society for the academic institution. It creates a platform for the students to show their talents, to enhance their knowledge, to combine the knowledge and action to achieve results for community development. Our NSS are committed to prepare the NSS volunteers for the democratic, self-disciplined and self-reliant way of life. NSS executive committee headed by the Principal and the committee members are the NSS programme officers, Chairman, Secretary and CEO.
